About the Game

Those Who Rule is a tactical turn-based RPG focused on positioning, terrain, and challenging combat. Lead a roster of unique characters, master over 40 classes, and forge your path through a land shaped by the choices you make.

  • Turn-based tactical combat with deep positioning mechanics
  • 24+ unique characters with distinct roles and motivations
  • 34 branching classes and 100+ abilities
  • Level up, change classes, equip custom gear, and hone your recruits into elite warriors
